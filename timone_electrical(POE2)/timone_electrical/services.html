Electrical Solutions

Comprehensive Electrical Services

Electrical Construction and Maintenance: We provide top-notch electrical construction and maintenance services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients, ensuring all installations meet the highest safety and efficiency standards. Certificates of Compliance (CoC): We issue CoCs for single-phase and three-phase electrical installations, confirming that your electrical setup complies with all regulatory standards and is safe for use.

Power Solutions and Safety

Generators and Compressors: We supply and install reliable generators and compressors, providing continuous power solutions for various applications, supported by ongoing maintenance services. UPS Systems: Our Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) services include supplying, installing, and maintaining UPS units to ensure your operations remain uninterrupted during power outages. Lightning Protection and Earthing: We offer lightning protection for low and medium voltage systems and ensure proper earthing and bonding to prevent electrical shocks and fire hazards.

Lighting, Ventilation, and Cable Management

Lighting and Ventilation Solutions: We design and install energy-efficient lighting and ventilation systems to enhance comfort and reduce energy costs for homes and businesses. Cable Tray Work: Our expertise in cable tray systems ensures organized and secure support for electrical cables, ideal for commercial and industrial environments.

Home Automation

Home Automation

Seamless Integration

Automated gates and garage doors, whether sliding or swinging, offer seamless integration with popular home automation platforms like Google Home and Alexa Home. This allows homeowners to control these entry points using voice commands or mobile apps, enhancing convenience and security.

Smart Control Options

With systems like Sonoff, Schneider, and CBI, homeowners can enjoy a range of smart control options for their automated gates and garage doors. This includes scheduling opening/closing times, receiving alerts for unauthorized access, and remotely monitoring the status of these entry points from anywhere.

Enhanced Security and Accessibility

Home automation enhances security by providing real-time notifications and access control features. Whether it's granting access to visitors remotely or receiving alerts for any suspicious activity, automated gates and garage doors integrated with smart systems offer a higher level of security and accessibility to homeowners.
